Divertimento #1

The first piece is titled Divertimento #1 composed by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. This piece required me to play high notes on the clarinet at a fast tempo (speed) with accuracy. However, after practicing this, I learned to master these skills and play the piece. This is one of the best pieces I have played on the clarinet.
